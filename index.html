Our Activities

Our Webinars are online meetings that invite top global experts in the energy sector to discuss and find solutions to global energy problems

  • You can always ask our guests questions directly or send them through our email prior to the session.
  • Everyone is welcome to our webinars, regardless of your level of expertise or education background
  • Use your phone to set reminders for our session so you don't miss any of our updates and impactful sessions
GET STARTED

Our Hackathons invite young people and university students to share ideas and any possible energy solutions they might have

Everyone is open to our hackathons. It is not limited to only engineering or science students at the university

Bring your ideas to life by participating in our intensive hackathons. Build energy solutions and pitch your ideas to thousands of energy enthusiasts

Our Forums provide you access to a community of energy enthusiasts who are willing to answer your energy-related questions

  • Leverage on our forum to learn everything you need to know about energy and the environment
  • Build strong professional relationships with other energy enthusiasts to share ideas and opportunities
  • Become a part of one of the top communities in the world advocating for change in the global energy sector
